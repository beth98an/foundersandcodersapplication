Why I am applying for this programme

Before starting my degree, I had no idea about what career I wanted to pursue after I had finished. But during my second year at university I discovered that I have a passion for computer programming when I was taught how to use python. Since graduating, I have decided that I would like a career in computer programming.

It would be a dream come true to explore and learn more programming languages and build on what I have already learnt from university and the self-teaching that I have done so far. Also, it would be great to meet other similar minded people with whom I can discuss programming, and I believe this course will give me the exciting opportunity to do this. Although programming can be frustrating and challenging at times, I find that the excitement and rush I get after I succeed makes it all worthwhile.
